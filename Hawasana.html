Hawasana (هوسانه)

Mantoo Ashak Bulani

Mantoo: Mantoo is a traditional Afghan dish made of dumplings filled with ground meat and spices, often served with a tomato-based sauce and yogurt.

Ashak: Ashak is a type of Afghan dumpling filled with leeks, and it is typically topped with a meat sauce and yogurt. It is a flavorful and satisfying dish.

Bulani: Bulani is a fried pastry stuffed with various fillings such as potatoes, spinach, or minced meat, offering a crispy and savory treat.

Mantoo Recipe

Ingredients: - Ground beef or lamb
- Onion
- Garlic
- Spices (turmeric, cumin, etc.)
- Flour (for dough)
- Yogurt
- Tomato-based sauce
Preparation: 1. Prepare the dough and filling with ground beef or lamb, onions, garlic, and spices.
2. Roll the dough and shape it into small dumplings.
3. Steam the dumplings until cooked.
4. Serve with yogurt and tomato-based sauce topped with fried onions.
Notes: Garnish with fresh cilantro for added flavor. Can be served with a side of salad.

Ashak Recipe

Ingredients: - Leeks
- Ground beef or lamb
- Garlic
- Flour (for dough)
- Yogurt
- Spices (cumin, turmeric, etc.)
Preparation: 1. Make the dough and prepare the leek filling with spices.
2. Shape the dough into small dumplings and stuff them with leek filling.
3. Boil the dumplings and top them with a meat sauce made of ground beef or lamb.
4. Garnish with yogurt and cilantro.
Notes: For a vegetarian option, use spinach instead of meat.

Bulani Recipe

Ingredients: - Potatoes or spinach (for filling)
- Ground beef (optional)
- Flour (for dough)
- Oil for frying
- Yogurt (for serving)
Preparation: 1. Prepare the dough and create the filling with potatoes, spinach, or minced meat.
2. Roll out the dough and fill it with the chosen filling.
3. Fry the filled dough in oil until golden brown and crispy.
4. Serve hot with yogurt or chutney.
Notes: Serve with a side of chutney or salad. You can also add cheese to the filling for extra flavor.
