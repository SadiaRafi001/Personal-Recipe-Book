Kofta Recipe

Ingredients:

  • 500g minced beef or lamb
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 1/2 cup parsley, chopped
  • 1 egg
  • 1 tsp cumin, coriander, turmeric
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 2 tbsp vegetable oil (for frying)

For the Sauce:

  • 2 tbsp oil
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2 tomatoes, chopped
  • 1 tbsp tomato paste
  • Salt, pepper, and 1 cup water/broth

Instructions:

  1. Combine minced meat, onion, parsley, egg, spices, salt, and pepper. Shape into small meatballs.
  2. Heat oil in a pan and fry meatballs until browned on all sides (5-7 minutes). Set aside.
  3. In the same pan, saut√© onions. Add tomatoes and cook until soft. Stir in tomato paste and spices. Add water/broth, salt, and pepper.
  4. Return meatballs to the pan, cover, and simmer for 20-25 minutes.
  5. Garnish with cilantro and serve with rice or naan.
