Sherni (شیرنی)

Sherni is a delightful assortment of traditional Afghan sweets, perfect for special occasions and celebrations.

Baghlawa


Baghlawa is a rich, flaky pastry filled with a variety of nuts and sweetened with syrup. It offers a crunchy and aromatic bite.

Khajoor


Khajoor are sweet dates often filled with nuts and sometimes soaked in sugar syrup, offering a chewy and satisfying texture.

Jelabi Rot Afghani


Jelabi Rot Afghani is a crispy fried sweet treat, shaped in intricate patterns and soaked in syrup, known for its golden, crunchy exterior.
