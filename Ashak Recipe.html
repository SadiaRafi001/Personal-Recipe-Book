Ashak (Afghan Chive Dumplings)

Ingredients

Ingredient Amount Unit
Chives or Leeks 2 bunches
Ground Beef 1 kg
Onion 2 pieces
Garlic 2 cloves
Dumpling wrappers 1 Pack
Yogurt 2 cups
Mint 3 tbsp
Cooked lentils 1 cup
Oil 4 tbsp
Salt & Pepper To taste

Cooking Instructions

  1. Chop the chives finely and lightly salt them. Set aside.
  2. Sauté chopped onion and garlic in oil, add ground beef and cook thoroughly. Season with salt and pepper.
  3. Fill dumpling wrappers with the chives and seal them tightly.
  4. Steam the dumplings for 25-30 minutes.
  5. Mix yogurt and mint to prepare the sauce. Set aside.
  6. Top the steamed dumplings with ground beef and cooked lentils.
  7. Drizzle with the mint yogurt sauce and serve hot.
