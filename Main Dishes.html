Main Dish (غذاهای اصلی)

Qabili Palaw

Qabili Palaw is a flavorful rice dish, typically served with lamb or chicken and topped with caramelized carrots, raisins, and nuts.

Mantoo

Mantoo is a traditional Afghan dumpling stuffed with minced meat and served with a delicious tomato-based sauce and yogurt.

Ashak

Ashak is an Afghan dumpling filled with leeks and served with a meat sauce and yogurt, offering a savory and satisfying taste.

Shorwa

Shorwa is a hearty Afghan soup made with meat, vegetables, and aromatic spices, perfect for a comforting meal.
