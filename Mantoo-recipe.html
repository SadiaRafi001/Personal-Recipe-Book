Mantoo (Afghan Dumplings)

Ingredients

Ingredient Amount Unit
Ground Beef 1 kg
Onion 2 kg
Garlic 2 cloves
Tomatos 1 kg
Dumpling wrappers 1 Pack
Yogurt 2 cup
Mint 3 tbsp
Oil 5 tbsp
Water 5 cup
Cooked lentils 1 cup

Cooking Instructions

  1. In a large pan, heat 2 tbsp of oil over medium heat. Chop 2 onions and 2 garlic cloves finely, then sauté them in the pan until the onions turn golden brown.
  2. Add 1 kg of ground beef to the pan and cook until browned, breaking up the meat as it cooks.
  3. Add salt and pepper to taste and cook the mixture for about 5 minutes.
  4. In a separate bowl, mix 2 cups of yogurt with 3 tbsp of mint to make the mint yogurt sauce. Set aside.
  5. Chop 1 kg of tomatoes finely or blend them into a smooth paste. Sauté them in a pan with 2 tbsp of oil for about 10 minutes, adding salt and pepper to taste.
  6. Take one dumpling wrapper, place a spoonful of the filling (ground beef) in the center, and fold the wrapper to seal. Repeat with the remaining wrappers.
  7. Steam the dumplings for about 25-30 minutes or until cooked through.
  8. Place the steamed mantoo dumplings on a serving platter.
  9. Spoon the tomato sauce over the dumplings.
  10. Sprinkle 1 cup of cooked lentils on top of the dumplings along with the sauce.
  11. Drizzle the mint yogurt sauce over the top for extra flavor.
  12. Garnish with extra mint leaves if desired and serve hot.
