Drinks (نوشیدنی)


Green Tea: A refreshing beverage made from the leaves of Camellia sinensis. Known for its health benefits, including antioxidants and aiding digestion.

Shor Chai: A traditional Afghan salty tea, often served with milk and a pinch of salt, providing a savory, soothing flavor.

Mango Milkshake: A creamy and sweet blend of ripe mangoes, milk, and a touch of sugar, offering a refreshing and nutritious drink.

Banana Milkshake: A delicious and filling drink made from bananas, milk, and sugar, offering a naturally sweet and healthy treat.
