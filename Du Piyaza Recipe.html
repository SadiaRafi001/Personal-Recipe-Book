Do Pyaza

Ingredients

Ingredient Amount Unit
Meat (Beef or Mutton) 1 kg
Onions (2 sliced + 2 chopped) 4 pieces
Tomatoes 2 pieces
Garlic 2 cloves
Turmeric 1 tsp
Garam Masala 1 tsp
Salt & Pepper To taste
Oil 4 tbsp

Cooking Instructions

  1. Sauté chopped onions in oil until golden brown. Add garlic and meat.
  2. Add tomatoes and all spices. Cook until meat is tender.
  3. In a separate pan, fry sliced onions until golden and crispy.
  4. Mix the fried onions into the curry just before serving.
  5. Serve hot with rice or naan.
